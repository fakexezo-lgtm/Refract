1. Big Problem: No Structure / Weak Information Architecture

Right now everything is just stacked:

Profile
Preferences
Account

That’s fine for MVP, but it won’t scale.

Why this is a problem

Users don’t “scan cards”, they scan categories.

Poor grouping is one of the most common UX issues in settings pages and makes navigation harder

Fix: Introduce Proper Settings Structure
Recommended structure (scalable):
Settings
│
├── Profile
├── Preferences
├── Notifications
├── Appearance
├── Tasks & Workflow
├── Account & Security
└── Data & Privacy
UI change:

Add left-side tabs (or top tabs) inside Settings page

This instantly:

Reduces cognitive load
Makes it feel like a serious product
Future-proofs your app
